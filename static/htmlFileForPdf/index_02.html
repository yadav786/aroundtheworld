Leverage any other visibility items (large chalk board, pop up banners,SNR). Cart set up in highly visible area, allowing for consumers to easily approach and enjoy their Baileys treat.
  • EVENT - Influencer proactively greets consumer with "would like to Treat Yourself? Try Baileys with coffee or over icecream" As the Influencer is discussing the versatility of BAILEYS with the consumer, they will serve a BAILEYS & ____sample (legal sample size) and offer information about the brand through active engagement that will drive the consumer to choose BAILEYS for their next occasion.
